To use:

It's off by default. On the command line assign vk= to one of these:

$11 (Play)
$12 (Reverse)
$13 (Forward)
$14 (Green)
$15 (Yellow)
$16 (Red)
$17 (Blue)

- Press the selected button to activate (it should print the character "a" to the screen)
- Press LEFT on DPad to backspace
- Press RIGHT on Dpad to enter new character
- Press UP or DOWN to cycle through selectable characters (does immediate backspace, waits 50 frames, inserts new letter)
- Press the selected button again to deactivate (this will also push an enter keypress)
